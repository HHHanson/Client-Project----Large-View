What to Include

“Include 2–4 high-quality case studies. For each, clearly present:
• Context & problem statement
• Your role, scope, and methods
• Key process artifacts (research plans, IA/flows, sketches, wireframes, prototypes, usability insights)
• Outcomes and impact (quantitative metrics and/or qualitative feedback)
• Reflection: what you learned and what you would improve next time.”
