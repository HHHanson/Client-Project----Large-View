Networking & Connections

Why Networking Matters

“Networking is about building relationships over time. It helps you learn about roles and teams, get feedback on your work, and find opportunities that rarely appear on job boards.”
— Generated with UM GPT

How to Get Started

“Begin with campus events and online presence, then request short informational interviews to learn and build genuine connections.”
— Generated with UM GPT

  • On campus: Attend CDO workshops, career fairs, and employer talks. Take notes and follow up with 1–2 people you met.

  • Online: Keep your LinkedIn updated; engage with posts from UMSI alumni and communities you care about.

  • Informational interviews: 15–20 minute chats to learn about someone’s work. Prepare 3–5 questions and always send thanks.

Sample Outreach Message

“Subject: UMSI MSI student seeking advice on UX Research
